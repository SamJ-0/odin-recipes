Pizza Recipe

A quick and delicious no-rise pizza dough made from simple ingredients.

A great recipe for a quick and easy pizza in no time!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups plain flour
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 pack of dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  1. Step 1:

    Prep all ingredients, including your desired toppings

  2. Step 2:

    Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
