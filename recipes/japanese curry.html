Japanese curry

Description

A simple and delicious mild curry.

Simple and hearty!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Curry roux
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  1. Step 1:

    Prep all ingredients, add oil to the pan and add onions once hot. Fry until soft and add chicken

    Once chicken is no longer pink add the veg and fry for a few mins.

  2. Step 2:

    Cover with water and boil whilst rice cooks.

    Once the potatoes are soft but not breaking apart, turn the heat off and add the roux and stir well
