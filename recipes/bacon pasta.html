Bacon pasta

Description

A quick and delicious pasta dish made from simple ingredients.

A great recipe for a quick and easy weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 10 rashers bacon, fat trimmed
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp worcester sauce
  • fresh basil
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • pasta
  1. Step 1:

    Prep all ingredients and add bacon to a hot pan, add onions once the fat starts rendering

    Once onions are soft add the garlic. Put the pasta on to cook.

  2. Step 2:

    Add chopped tomatoes once the bacon is golden. Add dry herbs, tomato puree and worcester sauce, mix well.

    Simmer until the pasta is cooked. Turn the heat off on the sauce and mix in fresh basil, serve.
